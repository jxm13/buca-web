Climate

Trees contribute to stabilizing our atmosphere by photosynthesizing and removing carbon. Currently up to 22kg of carbon per year can be secured by a mature tree

Social Impact

1.6 Billion people depend on forests for their livelihood. Planting trees therefore brings citizens closer naturally, heals communities and stimulates the local economies.

Biodiversity

For the 1/2 terrestrial flora and fauna worldwide and 3/4 of all birds, trees provide fruit, shade and shelter. They don't have nowhere to go when we chop them down.
